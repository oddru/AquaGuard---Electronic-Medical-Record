Common Waterborne Diseases

Cholera: A diarrheal disease caused by contaminated water.

Typhoid: A bacterial infection from contaminated food/water.

Dengue: A viral infection transmitted by mosquitoes.

Hepatitis A: A type of liver infection from ingesting contaminated water/food.
